Main Character "Eren Yeager"

Eren Yeager starts off as this kid who just wants freedom and hates feeling trapped. As the story goes on, he changes a lot and becomes way more serious and intense, doing whatever he thinks he has to do to protect his people.

The Walls and What They Represent

In Attack on Titan, the three walls aren’t just physical barriers — they represent fear, control, and how people limit themselves without even realizing it. The show uses the walls to show how a whole society can be kept in the dark, and how stepping outside your comfort zone is the only way to really learn the truth about the world.

Moral of the Story

The moral of Attack on Titan is basically that revenge and hate just keep making things worse. The show really shows how people get caught in a cycle of fighting, and the only way things get better is if someone finally decides to break that cycle.
