Main Character "Tanjiro Kamado"

Tanjiro is a gentle but determined character who never gives up on his family. Even after everything he loses, he stays kind and focused on helping others. His mix of strength and compassion is what makes him such a powerful protagonist.

Moral / Plot

The moral of Demon Slayer is that kindness matters, even in dark situations. The story shows how grief and pain can push people to grow stronger, but it also reminds us that understanding others even enemies takes real courage.

The World

The world of Demon Slayer is set in a historical era filled with demons, breathing techniques, and a secret organization of elite fighters. It mixes real Japanese culture with supernatural elements, creating a world where danger is always present but so is hope.
