Main Character "Goku"

Goku is a character who never stops trying to get better. No matter how strong he becomes, he always wants to push himself further, not just for himself but to protect the people he loves. His positive attitude and endless drive are what make him stand out.

Moral / Plot

The moral of Dragon Ball Z is that hard work and determination can overcome almost anything. The story shows that strength isn’t just about fighting it’s about loyalty, teamwork, and never giving up even when things seem impossible.

The World

The world of Dragon Ball Z is full of different planets, races, and huge battles that shape the universe. From Earth to Namek to entire other dimensions, the show creates a world where anything can happen, and danger can come from anywhere at any time.
